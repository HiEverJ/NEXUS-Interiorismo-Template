If you are looking for a static, predictable layout, look elsewhere. **NEXUS is massive fluid motion, pure volume, and uncompromising space curation.**

## 💎 Premium Signature Features (The 10,000/10 Standard)

*   **Liquid Glass v2 — Monolithic Sidebar Panel:** Strip away predictable top navigation bars. NEXUS introduces a translucent vertical spine (heavy matte glass blur with native backing drop shadows) that serves as the site's structural axis. It features intelligent scroll-direction contraction, mutating dynamically into a perfectly centered typographic initial to give the content absolute breathing room.
*   **Architectural Environment Switch:** Alternating between light and dark environments is reimagined through a custom component: a sleek, horizontal linear rail paired with a smooth micro-sliding node. Shift from "Deep Cement Charcoal" to "Warm Chalk Stucco" instantly, altering native root custom properties at 60fps.
*   **Interactive Material Curation (7 Author Hotspots):** A showcase canvas meticulously designed to sell your craft. Hovering over the environment activates 7 pulsing radar nodes that reveal high-contrast, blur-filtered micro-cards detailing raw finishes (travertine marble, smoked walnut, blued steel). Equipped with native bounding box protection to invert upward automatically near viewport edges.
*   **Cinematic Portfolio Track:** A massive, hardware-accelerated horizontal rail that emulates a cinematic camera tracking shot. Fully stabilized with minimal navigation anchors for desktop environments and native fluid inertial tracking for touch gestures on iOS and iPadOS.
*   **Fluid Editorial Typography:** The precise collision between classical Italian serif *Prata* and the structural geometric lines of *DM Sans*. Scaled mathematically via `clamp()` algorithms to lock golden-ratio proportions across all screens without stacking rigid media query overrides.

## 🏗️ Software Engineering Standards

*   **Modular SCSS Architecture:** Strictly decoupled component stylesheets so you can modify color tokens, custom glass mixins, and spatial grid constants in seconds without altering base structures.
*   **Zero Bloat / Pure Vanilla JS:** Driven by a lightweight, custom native script engine. Zero heavy tracking libraries, zero unoptimized frameworks, zero scroll lag.
*   **WebKit Optimization:** Specially coded to ensure flawless, flicker-free rendering and transition profiles across the Apple ecosystem (Desktop Safari, iPhone, and iPad viewports).
*   **Native Inline SVG Favicon:** Hardcoded directly into the document wrapper to entirely eliminate standard HTTP head requests on initial client-server handshake.
